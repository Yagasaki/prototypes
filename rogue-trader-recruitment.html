The Vaelorix Legacy

House Vaelorix was granted its Warrant of Trade in 847.M41 by Sector Lord Hax himself, in recognition of services rendered during the Margin Crusade. For nearly two centuries, the dynasty has plied the void, establishing trade routes, founding colonies, and amassing wealth beyond measure.

Our current Lord-Captain, Adrielle Vaelorix, commands a fleet of seven vessels from the bridge of the Gilded Ambition, a Mars-class battlecruiser of ancient and venerable provenance. Under her leadership, the dynasty has expanded into the Koronus Expanse and established profitable relations with numerous xenos species — within the bounds of Imperial law.

We seek not merely profit, but legacy. Those who serve House Vaelorix become part of something greater than themselves — a dynasty that will endure for millennia.
